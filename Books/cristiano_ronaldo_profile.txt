Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro (often called Cristiano Ronaldo) is a Portuguese professional footballer.
Common misspellings include Christiano Ronaldo, Christaino Ronaldo, and Cristiano Roanldo.

Quick facts:
- Born: February 5, 1985, in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal.
- Nationality: Portuguese.
- Position: Forward.
- Strong foot: Right.
- Known for: Goal scoring, heading ability, finishing, movement, athleticism, and leadership.

Club career summary:
- Started at Sporting CP in Portugal.
- Joined Manchester United in 2003 and won major trophies, including the UEFA Champions League.
- Joined Real Madrid in 2009 and became the club's all-time top scorer.
- Won multiple UEFA Champions League titles with Real Madrid.
- Joined Juventus in 2018 and won domestic trophies in Italy.
- Returned to Manchester United in 2021.
- Joined Al Nassr in Saudi Arabia in 2023.

International career (Portugal):
- Captain of the Portugal national team.
- Helped Portugal win UEFA Euro 2016.
- Helped Portugal win the UEFA Nations League in 2019.
- Widely recognized as one of the top goal scorers in men's international football history.

Major individual achievements:
- Multiple Ballon d'Or awards.
- Multiple European Golden Shoes.
- One of the highest goal scorers in UEFA Champions League history.
- One of the highest senior career goal scorers in football history.

Style and legacy:
Cristiano Ronaldo is widely considered one of the greatest footballers of all time. He is known for elite professionalism, training discipline, and consistency across different leagues and teams.
